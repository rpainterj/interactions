About Flightpath

The Flightpath is a neighborhood coffee shop and cafe at the corner of 51st and Duval Streets in Hyde Park

Flightpath serves great coffee and food to people who want peace and quiet. We have spacious seating indoors and outdoors, a full menu, and fast wi-fi.

We know that you're trying to get work done, so we don't have live music, poetry, comedy hours, or open-mic nights. Parking is available in front of the coffee shop or in the neighborhood nearby.
